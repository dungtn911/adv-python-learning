The 2019–20 coronavirus pandemic was confirmed to have spread to Vietnam
on 23 January 2020.[3][2] As of 21 April 2020,[update] there were 268
confirmed cases, 216 recoveries, and no deaths. More than 174,000 tests
has been performed. Hanoi is currently the most affected city with 112
confirmed cases.[1]
